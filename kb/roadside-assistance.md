# GEICO Emergency Road Service

## What's Covered (if you have this coverage)
- Towing (up to coverage limit)
- Jump start
- Tire change (spare must be available)
- Fuel delivery (you pay for fuel)
- Lockout service
- Winching/extraction

## How to Request Service
1. Call 1-800-424-3426 (24/7)
2. Or use the GEICO mobile app
3. Provide your location and describe the problem
4. Service provider will be dispatched
5. Typical arrival: 30-60 minutes

## Important Notes
- Must have Emergency Road Service on your policy
- Coverage applies per incident (limits apply)
- Driver must be present with vehicle
- Some services may have out-of-pocket costs

## Coverage Limits
Check your policy documents or call for your specific limits.
