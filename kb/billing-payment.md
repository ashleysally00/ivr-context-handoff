# Billing and Payment Options

## Payment Methods
- Online at geico.com
- Mobile app (iOS/Android)
- Automatic bank withdrawal
- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Amex)
- Check or money order by mail

## Payment Arrangements
If you're having trouble making a payment:
- We may be able to extend your due date (one-time courtesy)
- Set up a payment plan (eligibility varies by state)
- Adjust your coverage to lower premium (may affect protection)

## Late Payments
- Grace period: typically 10-20 days depending on state
- Late fee may apply
- Policy may lapse if payment not received
- Reinstatement possible but may require full payment
