# Filing an Auto Claim with GEICO

## When to File
File a claim as soon as possible after an incident. You can file 24/7 online, via mobile app, or by phone.

## Information You'll Need
- Policy number
- Date, time, and location of incident
- Description of what happened
- Photos of damage (if available)
- Other driver's information (if applicable)
- Police report number (if filed)

## Next Steps
1. A claims adjuster will be assigned within 24 hours
2. We'll inspect the damage (virtual or in-person)
3. You'll receive a repair estimate
4. Choose a repair shop or use a GEICO partner facility
5. Deductible applies based on your coverage

## Important Notes
- Filing a claim does not guarantee coverage
- Your premium may be affected depending on fault determination
- Rental car coverage depends on your policy details
